# Literature references

The PDFs of the foundational papers for this project were kept locally during development but are not redistributed in this repository. The citations below identify every source used in `docs/lit_review.md` and throughout the library documentation.

1. Allman, E. S. and Rhodes, J. A. (2007). Molecular phylogenetics from an algebraic viewpoint. *Statistica Sinica*, 17, 1299 to 1316.

2. Allman, E. S. and Rhodes, J. A. (2008). Phylogenetic ideals and varieties for the general Markov model. *Advances in Applied Mathematics*, 40(2), 127 to 148. DOI: 10.1016/j.aam.2006.10.002

3. Allman, E. S. and Rhodes, J. A. Lectures on phylogenetics from an algebraic viewpoint. Lecture notes.

4. Chifman, J. and Kubatko, L. (2014). Quartet inference from SNP data under the coalescent model (SVDQuartets). *Bioinformatics*, 30(23), 3317 to 3324. DOI: 10.1093/bioinformatics/btu530

5. Drton, M., Sturmfels, B., and Sullivant, S. (2009). *Lectures on Algebraic Statistics*. Oberwolfach Seminars 39, Birkhauser. ISBN 978 3 7643 8904 8

6. Eriksson, N. Using invariants for phylogenetic tree construction. Chapter in Pachter and Sturmfels (2005).

7. Pachter, L. and Sturmfels, B. (editors) (2005). *Algebraic Statistics for Computational Biology*. Cambridge University Press. ISBN 978 0 521 85700 0

8. Rhodes, J. A. Phylogenetic invariants. Chapter in Pachter and Sturmfels (2005).

9. Sturmfels, B. and Sullivant, S. (2005). Toric ideals of phylogenetic invariants. *Journal of Computational Biology*, 12(2), 204 to 228. DOI: 10.1089/cmb.2005.12.204
